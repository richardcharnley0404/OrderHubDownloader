AI Quality Gate

Score every image in every job before it dispatches to a printer. Jobs with any image scoring below the threshold are held for operator review and surface as a flag on the Jobs grid. No images are altered automatically — operators approve, fix, or reject from the held-job view.

Images scoring below this number are flagged for review. Default 50. Adjust based on your lab's print quality bar. Guidance:
• Below 50 — significant quality issues likely
• 50–70 — borderline; some labs accept, some don't
• 70–85 — typical phone uploads, usually fine for print
• Above 85 — high-quality images
When ON, jobs containing any image scoring below the threshold are held back from auto-print until an operator reviews them. When OFF, jobs auto-print regardless of scores (failed images are still flagged in Job Review).
